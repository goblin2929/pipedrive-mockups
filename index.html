PATTERN: Direct-Quote Hooks on YouTube Shorts
Validated across 6 data points • Confidence: High • Mar 15, 2026
Direct-quote hooks from named external experts outperform bold-claim hooks by 2.4x on YouTube Shorts. Effect strongest with personal vulnerability or contrarian insight. Does NOT hold for product demos or tutorials.
Direct Quote YT Shorts Vulnerability
Evidence: EXP-014, EXP-017, EXP-021 • Assets: Sean Evers, Daniel Disney, Troy Stevens
PATTERN: Video Posts on LinkedIn > Image Posts
Validated across 8 data points • Confidence: High • Mar 12, 2026
LinkedIn video posts (1:1 or 16:9, 60-90s) with thought leadership drive 3.2x more engagement than current image + text employer branding. Compounds when paired with a named individual (not brand voice).
Format Shift LinkedIn Thought Leadership
Evidence: EXP-003, EXP-007, EXP-011, EXP-019 • Baseline: 30 eng/post → 96 eng/post
NEVER: Generic Product Feature Announcements
Validated across 5 data points • Confidence: High • Mar 8, 2026
Generic "new feature" posts with product screenshots consistently underperform by 0.3x vs. average. Wrap features in a customer use case or problem-solution narrative — never lead with the feature name.
